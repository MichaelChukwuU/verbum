THE WAY YOU SHOULD REASON

If you dislike somebody, you exaggerate them in your mind. To exaggerate means you are unable to see things as they are, and if you are unable to see things as they are or as they are meant to be, you can’t handle life the way it should be. “A great many people think they are thinking when they are merely rearranging their prejudices.” — William James

The way one reasons has a lot to do with the decisions they make, the company they keep and most importantly, how they speak through their verbal and non-verbal communication. Finding yourself the right plug is very essential. You have to search much deeper within you and be sure you are positive minded.

Interestingly, but a sad truth is that you can never take someone up if they don’t want to. No wonder they say ‘when CRAZE meets GRACE you celebrate DISGRACE. Good intentions are useless in the absence of common-sense. The secret of your future is hidden in your daily routine. You are your best healer. Take up the responsibility in your hands that you will live well and that truly, is the only way to live well. Live a life you will remember. It starts with positive thinking and positive mindset.

‘You were born with the greatest weapon in all of nature- the rational, conscious mind.” Robert Greene
