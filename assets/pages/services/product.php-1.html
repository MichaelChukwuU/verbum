Parish Easy Software

The Parish Easy is a software designed by Verbum team to help your parish manage the data of your parishioners. Sometimes, management of data can be cumbersome, especially when you are using a manual database system or a poorly designed electronic database system. Verbum Parish Easy is designed to make data management easy and friendly. It makes data retrieval and statistical evaluation easy.
